About us

A small studio, built so the
work outlasts the launch.

We're a tight design studio working with founders, growing brands, and culture-makers. The team is small on purpose — every project gets a senior eye, every detail gets shipped on time.

Based in Berlin, working across Europe and the Gulf. We're picky about what we take on, and direct about the things we won't. Most of our work comes from clients we shipped for last year.
